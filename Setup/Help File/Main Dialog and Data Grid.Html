Main Dialog & Data Grid

    The "Main Dialog" or "Data Grid Dialog" is where TCP/IP packets will be displayed that were encountered but not filtered out by one or more of your Inclusion or Exclusion filters.



The data grid displays various columns:
    Date is the date that the packet was encountered.
    Time is the time that the packet was encountered.
    S-IP is the originating IP of the TCP/IP packet.
    D-IP is the intended destination IP of the TCP/IP packet.
    S-Port is the port that the TCP/IP packed originated from.
    D-Port is the intended destination port.
    Text This is the actual text data that was appended to the TCP/IP packet.
    Size This is the overall size of the TCP/IP packet.

The data grid also displays various icons for each specific data direction:
     Sent Packets are packets that were sent from your computer.
     Received Packets are packets that were received by your computer.
     Foreign Packets are packets that were encountered by your network interface that are not destined for your computer. These are usually packets that are broadcast from various broadcast protocols and data transmitted by older hubs (as opposed to switches).

Notes:
    The grid will only hold 100,000 lines of packet information. The oldest packets will be truncated from the grid as packets are received that overflows this limit.
